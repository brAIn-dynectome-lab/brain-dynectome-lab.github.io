Dr. Demitre Serletis

Principal Investigator

Dr. Demitre Serletis

Dr. Demitre Serletis, MD, PhD, is an epilepsy neurosurgeon-scientist in the Epilepsy Center, within the Neurological Institute at the Cleveland Clinic.

Dr. M. Rabiul Islam

Postdoctoral Researcher

Dr. M. Rabiul Islam

M Rabiul Islam, PhD is a biosignal informatics post-doc researcher at the Cleveland Clinic, where he develops AI-driven methods to analyze complex brain signals and localize epileptic networks. His work integrates signal processing, machine learning, and neuroscience to advance precision medicine in neurology and neurosurgery.

Neha Sara John

Senior Research Engineer

Neha Sara John

Neha Sara John is a Senior Research Engineer in the Cleveland Clinic's Epilepsy Center. She specializes in electrophysiology data analysis pipelines with a focus on multifractal and aperiodic analyses.
