BRAIN DYNECTOME LAB

SEEG Analysis • Brain Dynamics • Epilepsy Research

Demitre Serletis, MD, PhD

Principal Investigator

Leading computational neuroscience research on epilepsy dynamics using sEEG analysis.

The Dynectome Lab is led by Demitre Serletis at Cleveland Clinic.

We aim to find define high-complexity dynamical connectivity ('dynectome') profiles in stereoelectroencephalography (SEEG) data from drug-resistant epilepsy patients for applications towards surgical decision-making. We use theorectical and computational approaches.

We are particuarly interested in quantifying 'self-similarity', 'unpredictability' and frequency coupling in SEEG data.

We welcome curious students, collaborators, and clinicians interested in epilepsy research!
